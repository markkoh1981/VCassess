**Investibility Potential Report: Maiga.ai**

**Executive Summary:**
Maiga.ai is a decentralized AI agent platform that provides AI-powered signals, alpha, insights, and automated trading for DeFi (Decentralized Finance). The project aims to revolutionize the way DeFi protocols interact with each other by introducing an "AI Abstraction" concept. Our analysis suggests that Maiga.ai has strong potential for growth, but also comes with significant risks.

**Key Highlights:**

1. **Unique Token Model:** Maiga introduces a novel token model called "Proof-of-Trading," which is based on trading volume. This innovative approach aims to incentivize users and agents to participate in the ecosystem.
2. **Diversified Revenue Streams:** The project generates revenue through three main channels:
	* 0.3% fees from Maiga-ETH LP fees generated from trading volume
	* 1% fees for all AI Agent trading volume on Maiga.ai
	* 3.3% fees from the "Pot-as-a-Service" marketplace for other protocols
3. **Strong Partnership Network:** Maiga has partnered with over 50 web3 protocols and DAOs, including Techstars and Chainlink.
4. **Experienced Team:** The core team consists of experienced individuals, including Victor Lee (formerly of Huobi), Soon Lai (CTO at fintech in Singapore), and Cindy Jin (ex-IPG director).
